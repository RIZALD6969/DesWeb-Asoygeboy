About Business Economics Workshop (BEW)

Welcome to BEW, the premier platform nurturing the future leaders in Business and Economics in Southeast Asia!

Since 2018, we've sparked over 5,000 registrations, empowering hundreds of professionals and students towards groundbreaking business understanding. This year, Economics Research Institute and Global Business Forum proudly host the event, amplifying our dedication to nurturing innovation. Join us to unlock dynamic partnerships, as BEW shares its extensive resources to drive economic growth and collaboration across SEA and the world!!!

This event celebrates groundbreaking economic insights, featuring ten exceptional speakers and panelists from Southeast Asia, carefully selected from hundreds of experts. You will be able to witness visionary entrepreneurs pitch their transformative ideas to a distinguished panel of judges and industry leaders.

What Awaits You:

  • Inspiring Keynotes: Listen to leading economists and business strategists present their innovative solutions and cutting-edge economic theories.
  • Expert Insights: Engage in keynote speeches and panel discussions with top global investors, economic champions, and company leaders, gaining valuable perspectives on the future of innovation and the economic landscape in the Asia Pacific.
  • Networking Opportunities: Participate in our exclusive BEW Connect session to network with representatives from various industries and leading global venture capitalists.
  • Professional Connections: Meet and exchange ideas with fellow professionals from diverse industries across Indonesia to expand your network.

Join us for an inspiring day filled with innovative presentations accompanied by delicious Indonesian delicacies and coffee breaks. Let's celebrate the future of innovation together!

Please be advised as spaces are very limited, we suggest you register immediately.
